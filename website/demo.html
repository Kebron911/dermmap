Common questions before the demo

How long is the demo?

20 minutes. We keep it tight on purpose — 12 minutes of walkthrough, 8 minutes of Q&A.

Who should attend from our practice?

Whoever makes software decisions — often a physician-owner or practice manager. Sometimes an MA too, since they're the primary user. We'll accommodate any setup.

Do we need to prepare anything?

Nothing. We run a pre-built clinic scenario — you just need to show up.

Is this a sales call?

It's a product walkthrough. We'll tell you honestly if DermMap isn't the right fit for your practice — even if that means a "no" recommendation.

What happens after the demo?

If it's a fit, we'll walk you through a trial setup. Most practices are live within 48 hours.
