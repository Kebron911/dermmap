How It Works

From patient selected to lesion documented.

Select Patient

Open DermMap and search for the patient. Sync with your EHR at the start of day—or pull on demand. Offline mode available for exam rooms without Wi-Fi.

Patient List Icon

Tap Body Map

Interactive body map appears. Tap anywhere a lesion appears—head, neck, torso, limbs. Each tap marks a location. No freehand drawing required.

Body Map Icon

Snap Photo

Camera opens. Snap a photo of the lesion. Built-in focus and exposure guides ensure consistent clinical-quality images. Auto-timestamp and location tagging.

Camera Icon

Document

Add clinical notes: size, color, characteristics, assessment, plan. Pre-filled templates based on lesion type. Voice-to-text available for hands-free entry.

Documentation Icon

Visit Complete

Clinical PDF auto-generates. Marked for your EHR. Patient summary included. Ready to print or attach to chart in one tap. Longitudinal history tracked automatically.

The longitudinal view that changes how you follow patients.

Every lesion on one body map. Color-coded by status. Monitored, biopsied, excised. One tap to see the history of a lesion across months or years. No hunting through photos. No guessing if you saw that spot before.

What physicians get:

  • ✓ Complete body map overview in one view
  • ✓ Lesion history timeline: all photos, notes, assessments in chronological order
  • ✓ Color-coded status: monitoring (teal), biopsied (orange), excised (gray)
  • ✓ Comparison mode: side-by-side lesion photos across different visits

  • ✓ Export ready: clinical PDF or raw data to your EHR, audit-logged
