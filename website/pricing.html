Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge per patient or per lesion?

No. Flat monthly rate per practice, regardless of visit volume or lesion count.

What's included in "EHR integration"?

PDF export works with any EHR today. Our FHIR R4 integration roadmap covers Epic, Cerner, Athenahealth, and ModMed — the settings infrastructure is built, rollout is phased.

How long does onboarding take?

Most practices are fully live within 48 hours. The app is designed for zero-training adoption.

Is a BAA included?

Yes — a Business Associate Agreement is available with all plans.

Can I cancel anytime?

Monthly plans cancel anytime. Annual plans are non-refundable but include the 10% discount.
